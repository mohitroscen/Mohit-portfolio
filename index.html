Projects

Farm Surveillance Drone

This project features an advanced, AI-driven drone designed for large-scale farmland surveillance. Equipped with high-resolution cameras and powered by Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and Deep Learning, the drone autonomously flies over fast agricultural areas to monitor crop health in real time.

Trained on extensive datasets of healthy plants, the drone uses computer vision to detect even minor differences in leaves, fruits, or vegetables. When it identifies a potential defect or disease, it automatically descends to the affected area, captures detailed images, and sends a real-time alert to the farmer's mobile device along with a visual diagnosis and the name of the disease or issue.

This intelligent system empowers farmers with early detection, precise disease identification, and timely intervention, revolutionizing crop management and increasing productivity across a wide range of farming applications—not limited to any single crop.

HydroEnergen – Raindrop Energy Harvesting System

HydroEnergen is an innovative, piezoelectric-based system that converts the kinetic energy of raindrops and flowing water into clean, renewable electricity. By using piezoelectric transducer plates (like PZT or PVDF), the system captures mechanical impact from rainfall and transforms it into usable electrical power.

Designed for regions with frequent rain or near dam outlets, it features a modular and weatherproof architecture, robust energy storage, and a wireless IoT-based monitoring dashboard for real-time insights. HydroEnergen offers a scalable and sustainable alternative to conventional power, supporting smarter energy infrastructure and the global shift toward clean energy.
